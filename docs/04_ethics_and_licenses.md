# Ethics and licenses

## Data permissions
- Confirm whether scans can be used for research training.
- Confirm whether derived text outputs can be published.

## What we publish
- Code, configs, evaluation scripts, and aggregated metrics.
- Potentially a small "gold test" if permitted (or only its statistics if not).

## What we do NOT publish by default
- Full-resolution scans under restricted access.
- Large batches of extracted text if source forbids redistribution.

## Attribution
- Always cite the holding institution/source and follow their conditions.

## Sensitive content
- Historical documents may contain personal data.
- Prefer non-sensitive printed sources for initial phase.
